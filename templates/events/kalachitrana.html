RUPEKHA (Sketch Art)

Painting competition exploring themes through colors and creativity. Express your artistic vision on canvas.

ELIGIBILITY:
  • The time limit is 1 hour for each sketch art.
  • The event is open to artists of all levels who create artwork exclusively using pencils.
SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:
  • Artwork must be created using only pencils; no other coloring mediums are allowed.
ARTISTIC STYLE:
  • Participants are free to explore various artistic styles, techniques, and subjects as long as the artwork adheres to the pencil-only requirement.

Showcase your creativity and express your thoughts through painting in this artistic challenge.

Themes:
  • Theme 1: Save soil
  • Theme 2: Environmental Health
RULES:
  1. The student should be studying in UG/PG in the Academic Year 2024-25
  2. Category: Spray Painting, Oil Painting & Canvas Painting
  3. All artwork must be original; no entries will be accepted or considered if they are plagiarized.
