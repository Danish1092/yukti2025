HASTAKALA
(Painting)

Express your creativity and thoughts through painting on environmental themes. Showcase your artistic talent through various painting styles.

Themes:

  • Theme 1 - Save soil
  • Theme 2 - Environmental Health

Rules:

  1. The student should be studying in UG/PG in the Academic Year 2024-25
  2. Category: Spray Painting, Oil Painting & Canvas Painting
  3. All artwork must be original; no entries will be accepted or considered if they are plagiarized
  4. Only one entry per student
  5. The duration of the competition shall be one hour and thirty minutes. No extra time shall be given on any grounds
  6. The participants will need to BRING THEIR A3 SIZE SHEET OF PAPER (297 mm x 420 mm), drawing and painting material, and rough cloth for cleaning, etc
  7. Colour should not be thrown on the floor or side wall while executing the work or after the competition is over
  8. Evaluation will be based on the theme, quality, difficulty of work, artistic value, and overall presentations
  9. The event is open to artists of all levels who create artwork exclusively using pencils
